Spotify is an app that has gotten so popular over the years that Apple Music doesn't even compare but how did they get this website up and running in the first place?

The way it came into existence was from 2 people Daniel Ek and Martin Lorentzon who wanted a platform where people could listen to copyrighted music freely.
